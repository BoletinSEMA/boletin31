\noindent \textbf{Course}: \textit{\href{http://www.bcamath.org/en/courses/2023-01-16-bcam-course?utm_source=BCAM+Newsletter&utm_campaign=5399afa73d-EMAIL_CAMPAIGN_2018_10_31_10_33_COPY_01&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_f44359d1b6-5399afa73d-62270881}{An introduction to KAM and normal form methods for PDEs}}\newline
January 16, 2023 at 09:00 - January 20, 2023 - BCAM\newline
Riccardo Montalto (Università Statale di Milano)
\vskip1em

\noindent ABSTRACT: In this course I will give an introduction to the perturbative methods used in order to analyze the Dynamics of evolution PDEs, with a particular focus on dispersive PDEs and PDEs arising from fluid-dynamics (like Euler, Water Waves equations and some of their approximate models). More precisely I shall treat the following topics: 
\begin{enumerate} 
\item The problem of energy transfer to high frequency modes for linear Schr\"odinger type equations with time dependent potentials.
\item The problem of constructing quasi-periodic solutions for nonlinear equations (Kolmo\-gorov-Arnold-Moser theory for PDEs). 
\end{enumerate}
